As already mentioned, the Live Oaks in Galveston were all but lost. A group of home owners commissioned three Chainsaw Artist to make sculptures of what could be saved. They are amazing! I have uploaded a couple of pictures, but there is a brochure at the Visitors Center giving the address' of all the homes where they are located. It is worth your time to take the tour next time you are on the island.
