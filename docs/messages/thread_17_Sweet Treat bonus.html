From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-12-28 21:54:55 UTC
At Mercer Arboretum.
Pull into the west side, and take the first right into the Cypress
Pavilion parking area. Park by the pavilion.

See the restroom? Walk around the women's side and sit on the back
corner stone wall. Look to your left to a pine. Behind it you'll
find a special scoop.


Enjoy!
