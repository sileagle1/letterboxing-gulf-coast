So, am curious (not at all in a selfish way, oh no) what plans people
have for new boxes in 2007?

I have a couple of semi-series planned:
the Letterboxing Fools (Carving Fool, Finding Fool & Planting Fool)
Where in the World, wherein I get to play with different sorts of map
clues.
And I always wanted to do a series called The Bayou City (where I
plant one letterbox on each bayou around town), but so far I haven't
gotten up the steam for that one.
I will probably add some boxes to my Stop & Look series that so far
has one box, there's no place like home.
Probably some more seasonal, limited-time boxes (although I have a
hard time pulling them at the end).

I have a few other singles in the works, including some stamps that
have been carved for six months (or more!) but I haven't gotten
hidden. This year, I want to spend more time finding than hiding, but
I am the Carving Fool, so those stamps need to go somewhere.
