We have a new member, so everyone say hi to yippikiyo!

We've all been quiet lately, so thought I'd ask a question. . .

What's your preference, letterboxing out in the woods or letterboxing
at a park in the city?

I have to admit for myself, it's a seasonal thing. I like nothing
more than getting the boys out into the woods on a nice hike. . .but
in the summer around here (or during busy baseball season), that's not
so enjoyable/easy to do. So we save the shorter letterboxing
excursions for the summer & busier times.
