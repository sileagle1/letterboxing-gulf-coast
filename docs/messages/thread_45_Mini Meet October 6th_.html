From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2007-08-12 21:58:22 UTC
I was just checking our calendar for the next couple of months and
was reminded about the Mini Meet for October 6th. I went to AQ and
didn't find it posted. I'm assuming the event is still a go? We're
leaving it on our calendar just in case. I can't wait for cooler
weather. We went out and found a couple of nice boxes this morning,
but it's just too hot this afternoon. The boxes were Pastry
Princess's Muppets Series Gonzo box (great stamp) and Ghost Light of
Bragg Road. The latter is a nice box on an interesting road in
Hardin County, built on the roadbed of an old railroad, with
informational signboards along the way. It's been there since March
and we were first finders. I'm thinking of putting a box out there
just as a little more incentive to visit.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter
