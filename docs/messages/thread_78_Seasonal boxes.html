Personally, I'm going to try and do a holiday series this year again. 

Last year I did Holiday Lights at Memorial Park, that included a lot of the winter holidays.  I have a different project in mind for this year, but haven't had enough carving time recently.

I really like how Telge turned out--it's a great a group project because it's fun to be the planter and the finder.  And it keeps growing over the years...so there's always a reason to go back!
