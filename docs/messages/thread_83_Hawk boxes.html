Kokopelli, Batter Up!, Crook and Fleece, Hooked on Letterboxing, and
The Heights of Quaint.

Thanks dewberry and Barefoot Lucy(nical),

I hope The Fool is there, I wouldn't want to be the only one. Just
kidding. We wanted to go to 11th St. Park to get the Box of the
Month and were just looking for other boxes along the way, and Hawk
seems to have a lot still listed, although they appear to be kind of
strung out in various neighborhood parks that we are not familiar
with. We've pretty much got all the boxes in Memorial Park and
Mercer Arboretum and don't know of any other concentration of
boxes. We probably won't have a whole lot of time. By the way,
Lucy, thanks for the "dog" comments on the Texas board. There are
some possible "dog" letterboxes there that might fit the theme of
our "Dog Days at Double Lake" minigathering.

Boots Tex
