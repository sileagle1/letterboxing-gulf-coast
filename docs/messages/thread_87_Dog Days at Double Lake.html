Dog Days at Double Lake

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2008-04-01 21:39:44 UTC
We had a great time at the Dog Days event. Thanks, dewberry, for all
the work and organization. I don't know how you had time to carve all
those doggy stamps you put out at the pavilion and gather up the great
boxes that you placed out on the Lone Star Trail. We went out Monday
and found them all. We were all alone in the park and it was a very
relaxing time for us after a hectic weekend. I know you had helpers,
like MO5 and her LSS (thanks for the great hot dogs), as well as your
LSS, who seemed to be everywhere helping out. I think it's great that
they support your letterboxing activities like they do.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter

From: Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter (janet@redbarnretreat.com) | Date: 2008-04-02 14:42:46 UTC
After a hectic weekend with family and the event, walking along the
trail was a stress release for us, very relaxing, and all those boxes
were so beautiful. You're right, we couldn't just get one, we had to
get them all. Thanks for all you do for letterboxing, dewberry. If
anyone ever needs for us to check on a box for you, just let us
know. By the way, we moved LSQ's "The Hound's Tree" box. It was
right across from the big picnic area and we moved it to a more
remote spot, so reprint the clues if you come up this way. All of
you know where we live now, so if you're back in this area, you're
always welcome at the Red Barn.

Boots Tex and Lone Star Quilter
