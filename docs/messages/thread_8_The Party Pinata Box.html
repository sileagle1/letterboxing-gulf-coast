The Party Pinata Box

From: dewberrylb (dewberrylb@gmail.com) | Date: 2006-12-05 13:50:32 UTC
One of the things I've been wanting to do for a long time is a box
similar to Jay's Toolbox, a legendary box in Portland, OR

http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=9366&boxname=Jay's_Tool_Box

This is not dissimilar to the traveling Tour Bus, except it won't
travel, and it won't be people passengers--it will be candy & small
toys instead--the kind of things you find in a pinata.

What better way to launch a box witth sweet treats than to have it
available at the Candyland gathering?

We'll try to get out to Mercer and plant the box in advance of the
gathering, with some starter prizes & the pinata stamp. Unlike some
of the day's boxes, this one will be listed in advance. Finders are
encouraged (but not required) to bring along their own little treats
to add to the collection.

It'll count as one find, because that's what it is--one find with a
lot of stamps--but I'll try to keep the clues updated with the list of
current stamps in the box.

--Dewberry
